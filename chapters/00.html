Introduction and the Table of Contents

The Author, Qian Li, a multifaceted professional as an architect, urban designer, real estate developer and podcast influencer, complemented by the abilities in creative coding and programming. Qian holds degrees from Tsinghua University (B.Arch.) and University of Michigan (MUD), and has an experience of 19 years in the field with prestigious firms (Obermeyer, EE&K, Vanke) internationally (Germany, USA, China).

Beyond the realm of traditional urban studies, Qian draws insights from diverse disciplines including anthropology, geomorphology, economics, computer science and evolutionary biology, each offering perspectives on varied evolutionary systems.

This work is largely extracted from Qian's podcast channel :https://www.ximalaya.com/album/3733966 , which is originally in Mandarin language and is purposely serialized to later become a book on urbanism, which is temporarily named [An Evolutionary History of the City]. It underscores the self-organizational, bottom up generative aspects of the city and its problem awareness is set against the backdrop of contemporary China, where the convergence of 1) discourse and ideology that favors top-level design with 2) the needs of interest groups to seize wealth and power has resulted in highly authoritative urban mega-planning and irreversible damage to the built environment due to 3) professional malpractice. Nevertheless this work creates a few arguments/analyses on the broader history of urbanity, which the author expects to be innovational or even useful in the understanding of urbanism.

The content is in the process of being reorganized and refined to meet academic standards. The Author is working on re-writing/editing it in English, in order to improve its readability in English context. Various media, illustrations, charts and videos, are being created to make the work eventually visualized/animated.

这些作品是作者从2016年到2021年之间发布的播客中的一个系列作品。这些作品在创作的时候，就有意于形成一定的体系，并整理成为一本完整的关于城市演化的书。其中包含的理论框架较为庞大,而且雄心勃勃，除了受到现有城市学理论的影响之外，也从跨学科知识（经济学、人类学、地貌学、计算机学、生物进化学方面）得到启发，而表达出很多的创造性观点。

基于什么问题意识？作者基于他所处的中国房地产行业和推而广之的中国人造环境行业中的问题：1）毒化的话语体系对公众认知的污染，2）专家因自身的能力不足和利益关系而的失职，3）官僚机构的过度权力对公共资源的挥霍。
